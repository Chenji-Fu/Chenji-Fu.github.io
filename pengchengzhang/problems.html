It seems to me that there have been many conjectures or open problems in the field of modular forms, but it is often hard for young researches like myself to get to know about them. I have thus compiled this list, which is only restricted to open problems regarding the coefficients of modular forms, in hopes of spreading them to other young researchers in this field. Please feel free to let me know if you spot any mistakes, want to add more to the list, or are aware of existing solutions to any of them.

Maeda's conjecture first appeared as Conjecture 1.2 in a paper by Hida--Maeda. The strongest version of Maeda's conjecture is nowadays usually formulated as the following.

Conjecture 1. For all positive integers \(n\) and all weights \(k\) such that \(S_k(1)\) is nonempty,

  • the characteristic polynomial \(\phi_p\) of the Hecke operator \(T_p\) on \(S_k(1)\) is irreducible over \(\mathbb{Q}\), and
  • the Galois group of the splitting field of \(\phi_p\) over \(\mathbb{Q}\) is the symmetric group \(S_d\), where \(d\) is the dimension of \(S_k(1)\).
